  * AveragePool
  * BatchNormalization
  * Concat 
  * Conv 
  * ConvTranspose 
  * Flatten
  * GlobalAveragePool
  * LogSoftmax
  * MatMul 
  * Max
  * MaxPool 
  * Upsample
  * Relu 
  * Reshape
  * Sigmoid
  * Softmax
  * Split
  * Tanh
